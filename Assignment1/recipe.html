Guacamole Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados--peeled, pitted, mashed
  • 1 lime juiced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma tomatoes
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper *optional

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt.
  2. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.
  3. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  4. Refrigerate for 1 hour prior to serving.
