Our Story

We opened up 10 years ago to give folks a second chance to earn an honest living after exiting the correctional system. Naturally, we thought the most appropriate business in which we should employ ex-cons was in the auto sales industry, where we could test their mettle to see if they could resist identity theft when entrusted with the vital information of their customers. In 10 years, there have only been 2 breaches in security--almost guaranteeing your information is safe with us.
